About Us!

Our Family

KSPM FEB Unila is an organization that focuses on the study and development of the capital market. This organization operates under the auspices of the Faculty of Economics and Business (FEB) at the University of Lampung, located in Bandar Lampung, Indonesia. KSPM FEB Unila was established with the aim of enhancing students' understanding of the capital market, investment, and finance. The vision of KSPM FEB Unila is to create UKM-F KSPM FEB UNILA as a Center of Excellence for intellectually, creatively, and professionally capable students in the field of capital markets, with a strong sense of togetherness. KSPM FEB Unila strives to provide insights, knowledge, and practical experience to students regarding the world of the capital market
